Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Go to the UniThrift login page and sign up with your email or use Google Sign-In. Once registered, you can browse the marketplace immediately. To sell items, you'll need to complete student and seller verification from your profile.
Is UniThrift free to use?
Yes, completely free. There are no listing fees, no commissions, and no subscription charges. You keep 100% of what you earn from sales.
Who can use UniThrift?
UniThrift is built for students, faculty, and staff of Indian colleges and universities. You need a valid College ID to verify your student status and gain access to selling features.

Selling

How do I list an item?
Once seller-verified, click "Sell an Item" from the navbar or marketplace. Fill in the title, category, condition, price, description, and upload photos. Your listing goes live immediately after submission.
What should I price my item at?
A good rule of thumb is 40–60% of the original price for items in good condition, and 20–30% for fair condition. Check similar listings on the marketplace to gauge competitive pricing.
How do I mark an item as sold?
Go to your Profile → My Listings, open the item, and click "Mark as Sold". This removes it from the active marketplace and notifies the buyer if they were in a chat with you.
Where should I meet buyers?
Always meet in public, well-lit locations on campus — libraries, canteens, or college reception areas are ideal. Never share your home address with buyers you don't know personally.

Buying

How do I contact a seller?
Click "Quick View" on any listing and then "Message Seller". This opens a private chat room between you and the seller. All messages are real-time.
How do I pay for items?
UniThrift does not process payments directly. Payments happen between you and the seller — typically cash on handover or UPI transfer using the seller's QR code. Always confirm the item before paying.
What if the item isn't as described?
Inspect items before completing payment. If you believe a seller misrepresented an item, contact our support team with your chat logs and we'll investigate. Repeatedly fraudulent sellers are banned.

Verification

Why is my College ID being rejected?
Our AI checks that the uploaded document is a genuine College ID card — it must clearly show your institution name, your photo, and a student/roll number. Make sure the image is well-lit, not blurry, and shows the full card. Screenshots of digital ID apps may be rejected; use a photo of the physical card.
Why is my PAN card being rejected?
The AI looks for Income Tax Department branding, the 10-character PAN number (format: AAAAA9999A), your name, and date of birth. Upload a clear photo of the physical PAN card — not Aadhaar, not driving licence, not a screenshot of an app.
How long does verification take?
AI pre-screening is instant. Human admin review typically takes 24–48 hours. You'll receive a notification in your Updates feed once your verification status changes.
Is my PAN card data safe?
Yes. Verification documents are stored in a private Supabase storage bucket — they are never publicly accessible. Only UniThrift administrators can view them for review purposes.

Chat & Messages

I'm a seller — how do I reply to buyers?
Go to your Profile or the Chat page. All incoming buyer messages appear in your chat inbox. Open the conversation and reply directly — the same chat interface works for both buyers and sellers.
Why am I not getting message notifications?
Check your Updates page (/updates) for all notifications. Real-time push requires your browser to keep the tab open. If you're missing notifications, try refreshing your Updates page — they'll load from the server even if the real-time push was missed.

Account

I keep getting logged out — why?
Session tokens expire after a period of inactivity. UniThrift automatically refreshes them when you're active. If you're being logged out repeatedly, clear your browser's localStorage and log in again. If the issue persists, contact support.
How do I change my profile photo?
Go to Profile and click the camera icon on your avatar. Select an image from your device — it uploads automatically and updates across all your listings.
